About Us

Deep dive into RePLAN’s objectives, and long-term vision.

Reformation Practice and Legal Advocacy Network (RePLAN) is a non-profit dedicated to prison reform, legal advocacy, and supporting incarcerated individuals and their families. We work to equip inmates and ex-inmates with tools for reintegration, guided by justice, dignity, and global standards.

Our

Services

reintegration support

we prepare inmates for release by identifying their needs, reconnecting them with families, and linking them to job or business.

Support for Entrepreneurs

We help ex-inmates turn their skills into real income with mentorship, branding help, and access to funding.

Legal Reform & Advocacy

We provide legal aid, advocate non-custodial sentencing, and push to update outdated justice laws.

Training & Capacity Building

We train correctional officers and our team in restorative justice, advocacy, and sustainable reform strategies.

Public Awareness Campaigns

We run campaigns to inform the public on the effects of incarceration, reduce stigma, and support reintegration.
